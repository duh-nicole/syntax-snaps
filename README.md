🧐 What the he-double hockey sticks is a "Syntax Snap"?  
Syntax Snaps is my personal digital garden where I document the "aha!" moments of my coding journey.

Let's be real: Most technical blogs are dry, robotic, and a total snooze-fest. I created this mini-blog to bridge the gap between complex engineering and actual human experience. Think of it as a snapshot of a problem, a snippet of the solution, and the "story" of how I got there—usually involving a lot of coffee and at least one "waaah" moment.

---
🛠️ The Philosophy  
As a Data Evaluator and Storyteller, I believe that code doesn't exist in a vacuum. Whether it's a Bash script to fix my "morning brain" or a React component that actually follows WCAG standards, every piece of syntax tells a story.

My goal here is simple:

Demystify: Take the "scary" out of scripting.

Document: Keep a record of my growth as I finish my final quarter at MCC.

Humanize: Show the world that neuro-spicy, "loud-faced," snarky-grandma-loving humans belong in tech just as much as the robots.
